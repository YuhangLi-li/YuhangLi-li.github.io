Biography

I am currently working toward the Ph.D. degree at the Shanghai Film Academy of Shanghai University by Prof. Youdong Ding. During my Ph.D., I have visited the National Centre for Computer Animation (NCCA) at Bournemouth University as a visiting student advised by Prof. Xiaosong Yang. Prior to that, I received the Bachelor degree in the College of Computer Science at Zhengzhou University in 2019.

Research Interests:
Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence

  • Image & Video Enhancement:
    e.g., low-light enhancement, super resolution, frame interpolation, speech enhancement.
  • Image & Video Harmonization:
    e.g., light harmonization, color harmonization.
  • 3D Point Cloud Understanding:
    e.g., 3D object representation and reconstruction.
